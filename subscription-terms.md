MDViewer offers optional Pro access via auto‑renewable subscriptions and a one‑time lifetime purchase.

## Products

- Monthly Subscription — renews every month
- Yearly Subscription — renews every year
- Lifetime Purchase — one‑time payment, no renewal

Actual product IDs and prices are displayed in the App and may vary by region and currency.

## Payment & Renewal

- Payment is charged to your Apple ID account when you confirm the purchase.
- Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the current period ends.
- Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

## Manage or Cancel

- You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID settings: App Store → Profile → Subscriptions.
- Canceling a subscription takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Access remains active until then.

## Price Changes

Prices may change. If so, Apple may notify you and request your consent to continue. If you do not consent, your subscription may be canceled at the end of the current period.

## Refunds

Refunds are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

## Restore Purchases

If you reinstall the App or switch devices, use **Restore Purchases** in the App to re‑apply your active entitlements.
