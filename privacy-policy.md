# MDViewer Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-09

MDViewer (“the App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the App handles and how.

## Summary

- The App does **not collect**, **store**, or **share** any personally identifiable information.
- Photos permission is requested **only** to save exported images you explicitly create.
- In‑App Purchases are processed by Apple; the App does not receive your payment details.

## Data Collection

- The App does not implement analytics, advertising SDKs, or third‑party trackers.
- No personal data is transmitted to external servers by the App.

## Photos Permission

- Purpose: When you export a document as **PNG**, the App requests access to add that image to your **Photo Library**.
- Scope: The App does **not** read, upload, or analyze your existing photos. Only the exported image is written.
- You can deny this permission; in that case, PNG export to Photos will not be available.

## Files Access

- When you open a `.md` file from WeChat or the Files app, the App saves a copy into its in‑app library so that you can preview, edit, and export it later.
- These files remain on your device unless you delete them from within the App.

## In‑App Purchases

- Subscriptions or lifetime purchases are handled by **Apple** via the App Store.
- The App does not process or store payment information.
- You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.

## Children’s Privacy

- The App is not directed to children under 13.
- The App does not knowingly collect any personal information.

## Security

- Because the App does not collect or transmit personal data, typical server‑side security risks do not apply.
- Files and exports remain on your device unless you choose to share them through the iOS share sheet.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page.
